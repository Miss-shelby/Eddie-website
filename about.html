Education and Early Development

Mr. Edemakhiota is deeply rooted in his Edwin began his formal education at Zion International School, Onitsha, where he completed his primary education. In 2005/2006, he enrolled at Mater Christi Secondary School, Iba Pope Awada, Onitsha, where he completed his secondary education in 2011.

During his formative years, Edwin showed a natural inclination toward business, creativity, and problem-solving. These early traits laid the foundation for his entrepreneurial mindset and his determination to build sustainable ventures.

Early Entrepreneurship and Setbacks

Mr. Edemakhiota is deeply rooted in his At just 16–17 years old (2010–2011), Edwin launched his first business venture, marking the beginning of his entrepreneurial journey. Driven by passion and ambition, he opened a salon business, gaining early exposure to customer service, business operations, and financial responsibility.

Despite its initial promise, the business faced challenges and ultimately closed in 2015. Rather than being discouraged, Edwin viewed this experience as a critical learning phase—one that strengthened his resilience, sharpened his judgment, and prepared him for greater opportunities.

Relocation, Corporate Experience, and Betting Business

Following this setback, Edwin relocated to Benue State, where he strategically repositioned himself for growth. He joined KC Gaming Network, a betting and gaming company, where he worked within a structured business environment. This role enhanced his understanding of operations, customer relations, compliance, and daily business management.

Building on this experience, Edwin took a decisive entrepreneurial leap. In November 2016, he opened his own betting shop and became one of the stakeholders in the business. This milestone marked a successful comeback and reinforced his reputation as a determined and adaptable entrepreneur.

The Birth of Mastercutz Barbershop

With renewed clarity and industry experience, Edwin returned to the grooming industry with a refined vision. On October 3, 2021, he officially founded Mastercutz Barbershop, a brand built on professionalism, premium service delivery, and strong operational standards.

Mastercutz Barbershop quickly evolved into a trusted name in personal grooming, expanding its footprint and establishing operational locations in Awka and Abuja. The brand became the cornerstone of a growing business ecosystem.

Professional Training and Expansion into Cleaning Services

Committed to excellence and structured growth, Edwin pursued formal professional training in the cleaning industry. He attended Chi Kleen Cleaning Services Academy, where he received hands-on training in the ethics, standards, and operational systems of professional cleaning and laundry services.

Armed with industry knowledge and best practices, Edwin launched Master Laundry and Cleaning Services in 2023, offering reliable, hygienic, and professionally managed laundry and cleaning solutions. The brand operates under the same values of quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction that define the Mastercutz ecosystem.

Mastercutz Empire Limited and Technology

Edwin is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Mastercutz Empire Limited, the parent company overseeing:

  • Mastercutz Barbershop & Spa
  • Master Laundry and Cleaning Services

Beyond entrepreneurship, Edwin is also a software developer, leveraging technology to build scalable systems, digital platforms, and efficient business processes. His ability to merge technology with practical business experience positions him uniquely as a modern, innovation-driven entrepreneur.

Personal Life and Philosophy

Edwin Osagie Edemakhiota is a husband, a disciplined businessman, and a lifelong learner. He is deeply rooted in his cultural heritage and driven by a desire to build a legacy defined by integrity, excellence, and impact.

Mr Edemakhiota’s lifelong philosophy is that structure precedes scale. Systems create freedom. Discipline compounds and growth with foundation is temporary.
